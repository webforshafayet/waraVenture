Questions & Answers

Why should founders choose turtle venture studio to build their venture?
Turtle venture studio would provide all the necessary services to their selected portfolios, including product development, ensuring product-market fit, scaling products in the right way, expert advisory, onboard the right team, founder grooming classes, demo day in Singapore, company registration in Singapore, Support from partner organizations, networking opportunity, Investment raise support, and many more. As a result, all the graduated founders will end up building a successful global venture and be able to make their mark. The benefits of being part of a venture studio are endless.

Do I need to have a Minimum viable product (MVP)?
Yes. You must have some form of MVP to be eligible for the application. The MVP serves as a prototype of the product or service and enables the founder to obtain feedback from the target audience, validate their assumptions, and make informed decisions about the product's future development. Therefore, the presence of a viable MVP is a prerequisite for being eligible for the application process.

Can I apply for more than one portfolio?
To be considered eligible for the Venture Studio Cohort, a founder is restricted from submitting applications for multiple portfolios. Additionally, they must solely focus on the development of a single startup to be eligible for the program.

Is there any age requirement as part of the eligibility criteria?
Yes. All founders must be 18 years old and above.

Should I apply for this program if I’m actively raising funds?

Yes. if you get selected, our team will start working on helping you raise the fund for your startups immediately.

Will my company be registered in another country apart from Bangladesh?
Yes. After six months of successfully building the product and achieving minimum market tractions, your company will be registered in Singapore to attract ultimate opportunities.

Why should investors invest in Turtle venture studio portfolios?

Turtle venture studio will make sure all the portfolios from the venture studio have good market traction, founder and the team has a great understanding of the business and market,and all the portfolios have good product market fit. All of these matters would enhance the possibilities of the highest return for the investors and reduce the failure rate significantly.
